Professional life

My professional life started with an automotive engineer career.When working as an engineer in automotive industry you are always connected to the latest technologies in this area which can be very satisfying.As expected, i graduated the Faculty of Transports from the Polytechnic University of Bucharest.If i try to look back, i understand better that this kind of university besides the fact that specializes you in a certain area it also gives you the power of logical thinking.

Why did i choose to change my career path?

As a developer you can consider that your perspectives are unlimited.There is always something new to learn,something new to experiment,something new to test.This empowers your brain to remain active, a great way to keep your physical and mental condition at a high level.The enthusiasm for doing this comes from an old passion for programming and developing things.
